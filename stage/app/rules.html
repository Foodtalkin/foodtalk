Rules of use

OFFERS

Each restaurant partner will provide discount coupon(s), that will be valid for a period of one year from the date of purchase.

Each coupon entitles the user to :

One complimentary buffet, appetizer, main dish and cocktail when another similar item is ordered of same or lesser value.

OR

15% discount on bill when food, alcohol or hookah is purchased

OR

20% discount on only Food bill.

To know other options of offers available to you, please refer to the offer details of your chosen restaurant.

The offer applies to one bill per table/group. At one time, at one restaurant, ONLY a maximum of 3 Food Talk Privilege coupons can be redeemed

Please Note:
Coupon redemption is only valid on ONE bill per table.
Two offers at the same restaurant cannot be clubbed together in one meal.

The user should ensure that the Management of the restaurant is aware and informed in advance Food Talk Privilege coupon(s) will be used.

The Food Talk Privilege App coupon(s) must be presented and redeemed prior to requesting for the bill.

If the App is presented after the bill is generated, restaurant will not be liable to provide deal or offer.

Coupon(s) apply only to the items and terms specified on the offer. Please read the Offer Details for your chosen restaurant in the app

Offers can be used at any time during regular opening hours, unless specified.

Once coupon redemption is authorized by the merchant there is no way to cancel it in the system and the redeemed coupons will be deducted from the user's Food Talk Privilege App account.

Users may get other promotional offers for a limited period during the validity of the app, which may run from time-to-time.

Please note Food Talk is not responsible if an outlet is temporarily or permanently closed or is no longer a partner on Privilege. Please check the coupon and restaurant validity before planning your meal.



EXCLUSIONS

The Food Talk Privilege App will not be applicable on the following days:

  • Christmas Eve
  • Christmas Day
  • New Year's Eve
  • New Year's Day
  • Valentine's Day

All offers are exclusive and not valid in conjunction with any other discount offers, promotions, special menu items, and dining or loyalty programs

Offers are valid solely ON the item listed and are not valid for special events, theme nights, buffets or brunches - unless specified.

Offers do not apply to Breakfast, Small Plates, Shared Platters, Side Dishes, Rice and Breads, Desserts and Beverages (alcoholic or non-alcoholic), unless specified otherwise.

Offers are not valid in conjunction with B.Y.O (bring your own liquor) or Corkage Fees.

Coupon(s) are not transferable and are void if purchased, sold or bartered for cash.



The Food Talk Privilege App coupon(s) are valid for dine-in only, unless specified.

Offers are not valid for delivery, unless specified.

Discounts do not apply to Service Charges and/or Government Taxes

Please note, that the Food Talk Privilege App is not responsible in the event of an outlet or restaurant being temporarily or permanently closed during the offer validity period.
