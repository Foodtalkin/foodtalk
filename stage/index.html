Yin & Yang of Food Talk


Shuchir Suri
Founder, CEO

Founder and CEO and a serial entrepreneur who established himself in the marketing arena since his early college days. Backed with years spent in marketing, strategy and product development, Shuchir heads the Operations & Digital vertical of the company. Shuchir is a food aficionado and alcohol evangelist who has been making waves in the country’s food and drink scenario with a powerful online and offline presence. With meticulous planning and ability to effortlessly multi-task Shuchir has pulled off large scale events pan India partnering with leading brands. He has also emerged in the beverage industry with the inception of another self-created brand called Jade Forest Beverages. He can be found mixing up the best cocktails as meticulously as he organises his work. He is the glue that binds the team together.

Anjali Batra

The creative half of the team and the curator of immersive experiences, Anjali comes with a plate full of marketing knowledge and PR, served with a generous side of consumer behaviour understanding, Anjali is responsible for curating all the Food Talk experiences. Forbes 30 Under 30 Asia, Class of 2018 is an added feather to her cap along with being the brainchild behind India's largest Gin festival- Gin Explorer's Club founded in 2018.
