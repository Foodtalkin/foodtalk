Gin Explorers Club

Gin Explorers Club
A concept by Food Talk India

Gin Explorers Club is a festival and club for Gin Lovers.

A weekend long celebration of Gin - the Gin Explorers club is a festival that showcases a selection of Indian & International Gin Brands all under one roof for Gin Lovers and enthusiasts to explore. With Pop-Up bars and immersive tasting sessions - guests can discover new gins and sip on some fabulous cocktails.

Packaged together with some of the biggest music acts, entertainment and an array of Pop-Up restaurants – GEC is a weekend long celebration centered around the spirit Gin.
