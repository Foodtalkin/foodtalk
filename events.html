About our events

We believe that when an online community of passionate and hungry souls interacts with each other over a great meal offline, explosive things happen.
Shaking up the Food space with one-of-a-kind curated experiences- the Food Talk Events are a melting pot flavours, cocktails and great company - brought together with a little Food Talk creativity.

Hop onto an unparalleled Food Talk journey each time with idiosyncratic themes and anomalous concepts. Indulge in enticing conversations, gluttony and good times by all. A quintessentially Food Talk celebration of flavours.

You’ll have to attend one to know why everyone wants a ticket.
