About Us

Food Talk is a food loving technology company that uses location intelligence to build meaningful consumer experiences and business solutions. We are an App and a soon to be launched advertising tool.

For consumers, we believe the world is full of amazing experiences and are trying to make finding good food a visual experience.

The Food Talk App helps you discover new places, with recommendations from a community you trust. Hoping to cover every corner of the world using this community.

For restaurants & brands, Food Talk provides advertisers ways to connect with audiences that love the things they offer, in the real world. Our Tool optimizes targeting and uses first-party location data to ensure accuracy and quality.

Community: More than 500k people use or follow us across desktop, mobile web and digital.

Team: We are a small team of 12 based out of Gurgaon, Haryana, India.
