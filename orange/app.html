About our App

Behold, an App for all app-etites. Say hello to the Food Talk App

Discover | Eat | Share

Revolutionising the way you talk food.

Why read what to eat when you can SEE and make a decision?
With a gallery of rated dishes (courtesy fellow FoodTalkers) , the Food Talk app is your very own Curated Food Guide. Decide from drool-worthy photo reviews shared by other foodies and discover a great meal, instantly.

No more long reviews! Food Talk is an easy and instant platform to discover new restaurants, dishes and experiences via fellow Food Talkers.
A Social Network For Good Food
The country’s most trusted authority on deliciousness Food Talk India, brings you a platform where it takes all of 7 seconds to Discover, Eat and Share good food.
