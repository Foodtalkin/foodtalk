Rules of use

OFFERS

Each participating restaurant will provide single-use discount coupons, which will be valid for one year from the date of purchase.

Each coupon entitles the user to one complimentary buffet, set meal, main dish, IMFL drink or cocktail when another similar item is ordered of same or higher value. OR 15% discount on bill when Food, alcohol or Hookah is purchased and 20% discount on bill when Food is purchased.

TO KNOW WHICH OFFER IS APPLICABLE PLEASE SEE THE OFFER DETAILS FOR THAT PARTICULAR RESTAURANT.

The offer applies to one bill per table/group. A maximum of 3 Food Talk Privilege e-vouchers can be redeemed at one time at one restaurant.

The table below explains how many coupons can be redeemed:

Diners 2-3 4-5 6+
Eligible items ordered 2-3 4-5 6+
Coupons allowed 1 2 3

The user should ensure that the Server/Manager of the restaurant is aware that they will be using the Food Talk Privilege App e-voucher.

The Food Talk Privilege App e-voucher must be presented and redeemed prior to requesting the bill.

Coupons apply only to the items and terms specified on the offer. Please read the Offer Details for your chosen restaurant in the app or on our website before dining there.

Offers can be used, at any time during regular opening hours, unless specified.

Once a redemption is authorized by the merchant there is no way to cancel it in the system and the redeemed coupons will be deducted from the user's Food Talk Privilege App account.

Users may get other promotional offers for a limited period during the validity of the app, which may be run from time-to- time.



EXCLUSIONS

The Food Talk Privilege App cannot be used on the following days:

  • Christmas Eve
  • Christmas Day
  • New Year's Eve
  • New Year's Day
  • Valentine's Day

E-Voucher are not transferable and are void if purchased, sold or bartered for cash.

All offers are exclusive and not valid in conjunction with any other discount offers, promotions, special menu items, and dining or loyalty programs.

Offers are valid solely for the item listed and are not valid for special events, theme nights, buffets or brunches - unless specified by the merchant.

Offer does not apply to Breakfast, Small Plates, Shared Platters, Side Dishes, Rice and Breads, and Desserts (alcoholic or non-alcoholic), unless specified otherwise.

Offers are not valid in conjunction with B.Y.O (bring your own liquor) or Corkage Fees.

Discounts do not apply to Service Charges and/or Government Taxes

The Food Talk Privilege App e-voucher are valid for dine-in only, unless specified.

Offers are not valid for delivery, unless specified.

Please note, that the Food Talk Privilege App cannot be held responsible if an outlet is temporarily or permanently closed during the offer validity period.
