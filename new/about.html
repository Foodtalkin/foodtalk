Story

Something Different, Something New.

We started our journey 3 years ago as a small, 50 people invite-only Facebook groupSharing food experiences, getting recommendations on what to order, recipes, reviews – in a nutshell, all things Food.
And all in real time.
But the hunger was insatiable and our love for food, limitless.
Fast forward today, we are 500000 + Food Talkers and growing stronger
Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, Snapchat – take your pick and come get Food Talkin’ with us!
If that isn’t enough, we also curate some fantastic Food Talk events- because great things ensue when people and the food they love come together and we exist to make that happen.
Come get a piece of us, wherever you like

our team.

We are a small bunch of passionate individuals who believe that food makes the world go round. Allow us to introduce ourselves.
