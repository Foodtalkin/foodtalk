What We Do

FoodTalk is the country’s largest F&B platform, with an actively engaged audience of over half a million people.
Armed with experience in the culinary business and knowledge about innovative marketing, trends and technology, we design strategies that cultivate maximum returns.
Our mission is simple – to ensure your brand reaches towering heights. And our modus operandi includes targeted advertising to a relevant audience through disruptive, novel marketing methods.
We work with brands and businesses to help them share their story in the most effective way possible.

Here’s what we bring to the table

Advertising

Our expertise lies in innovative advertising tools which help your brand to achieve the highest level of customer engagement. We have what it takes to ensure you speak to an audience that is right via hyper-local, real-time, targeted advertising.

Brand Promotions

Reach out to us for out-of-the-box online and offline marketing strategies that promote your brand in the most effective way possible. No one understands better that in today’s world everything is passé as soon as it is created, and we strive to reinvent our strategies continually.

Digital Services

We offer a gamut of digital services including influencer marketing, content creation, collaborations, digital video content, blogger management, campaign creation, product launches and much more to make the noise your brand needs.

Events & Experiences

From immersive dining experiences, to handcrafted cocktail and nightlife experiences, we seamlessly blend your brand strength's and story to create one-of-a-kind experiences. We work with brands to curate bespoke events that amplify reach and build awareness.

Privilege Partnership

Increase your restaurant's footfall, revenue and exposure by coming on board as a partner of our exclusive member only dining club - FoodTalk Privilege. Enhance your reach as your get access to the country's largest community of food lovers under one roof.

Corporate Services

From product launches to team-building activities, we come in and create bespoke experiences in the F&B space. From Michelin star Chefs, ace mixologists, cocktail making workshops to custom coffee blends we do it all. with a network that consists of the best names in the industry, rest be assured you’ll be the Talk of the town.
