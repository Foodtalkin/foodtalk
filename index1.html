Food Talk is a visual platform to discover, rate and share great dishes.

Food Talk is a visual guide to good food and where to find it. Instead of reviewing restaurants, you can recommend and rate dishes and see what others recommend wherever you go. We are trying to make finding cravings easy and making finding good food a visual experience.

On Food Talk takes less than 5 seconds to click, upload and rate a dish one loves which enables Food Seekers to find whatever they are craving or see what’s good at the restaurant. It's visual. Say bye to boring long reviews and Say hello to Food Talk.
