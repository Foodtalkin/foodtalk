About Event

An interactive 4-course wine paired menu- to have an unforgettable experience with the people you cherish.

Taking the edge of a traditional Wine tasting- we guide you through each wine in a most unconventional and interactive way- as you enjoy a beautiful meal in the company of your friends.

So come join us for a little Eat, Drink and Play - as you catch up with old friends.

Price for the dinner: Rs 1,500 Ai per head.
Booking fee: Rs 300 per head.
{Table booking is recommended- please make a reservation for all friends joining you}

PS- we have a few special tricks planned up our sleeve. Be prepared to get your hands a little dirty.

After all, they say the best wines are the ones we share with friends…
Wine Made by us, Moments made by you.
See you at Our Table.

ABOUT JACOB’S CREEK RESERVE

Each of our Reserve wines is made from high quality grapes sourced from a single region, renowned for that grape variety. With a focus on Barossa, Adelaide Hills and Coonawarra, Jacob’s Creek Reserve celebrates provenance by reflecting the unique personality of each region in the wine.
