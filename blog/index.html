MasterChef’ing our way to Bangalore!

A Gourmet experience like never before- quite literally! Because we are heading to Bangalore for a MasterClass with MasterChef Australia Judge Gary & Chef Pankaj at one of the most awaited international food festivals- World On A Plate.

And the best part- we might just be talking YOU along with us.

All set to happen at the JW Marriott, UB City  this June the festival has some super exciting things lined up for all us gourmands out there!

With over 50 restaurants from across Bangalore setting up stalls- we look forward to getting a taste of a wide array of innovative dishes #FoodGasm here we come!!

Oh wait, did we forget to mention the best part?? The oh-so-loved MasterChef Australia judges, Matt Preston, Gary Mehigan and George Calombaris are going to be there And...wait for it...they will each be setting up a stall and serving up a gastronomic delight!**Gasps Breath**

Jealous Much?? Well wait till you hear the next part ;)

If Knorr wasn’t adding enough flavour into our lives with all their fabulous range of Masalas they just upped the flavour quotient to the power of...George.

We'll be attending an exclusive #KnorrMasterClass with Gary Mehigan, the fabulous MasterChef India season 1 winner Chef Pankaj Bhadouria as they teach us tips and tricks to create gastronomic magic using the amazing Knorr Masala range.

Better yet- they want us to select one of you to join us in Bangalore with a friend for this Super exclusive #KnorrMasterClass with these two MasterChef maestros AND an all expense paid trip for two to attend the to the WOAP festival in Bangalore!

Excited? Well all you gotta do is participate in the Knorr contest and you might just stand a chance to win that golden ticket:

  1. Create - An innovative dish using any Knorr Chef’s Masala
  2. Click a picture of the dish with the Knorr Chef’s Masala packet
  3. Upload the picture on the Food Talk India Facebook page using the hashtag #KnorrMasterClass.

You’re done! Winners will get to attend an exclusive  #KnorrMasterClass with MasterChef Australia Judge Chef Gary and MasterChef India Winner Pankaj in an all expense paid trip, in Bangalore with a friend!
